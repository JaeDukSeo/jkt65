John Tsotsos recently turned 65! To honour John’s many contributions in the visual sciences and longevity, we are holding a one day event — a verbal Festschrift if you like — on Saturday May 12th, 2018 at York University in Toronto, Canada. We will have a number of talks from distinguished researchers in areas in which John has contributed, as well as a number of talks by his academic children.

We are currently finalizing the details for the event, e.g., coffee breaks, name tags, and seating. We ask you to please RSVP below.

If there is any further information that you might need, do not hesitate to contact Kosta Derpanis at kosta@scs.ryerson.ca. John’s Festschrift is being held the day after AI/GI/CRV which is also being held at York University.
